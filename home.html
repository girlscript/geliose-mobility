WHO ARE WE

About Us

  • At Geliose Mobility we believe that the future of the commute is shared, connected and electric. With this vision, we are working towards developing an electric vehicle ecosystem to support public transport.

  • To accelerate the transition towards zero pollution commute, we are focused on developing core technologies that run and supports electric vehicle architecture.

  • With the application of developed technologies, we are bringing innovative and efficient vehicles to life to solve the existing problems of public transport.

  • Together our products and technologies will form such an ecosystem that will help solve a bigger cause of fighting the rise of vehicular pollution and subsequent climate change.





PEDICAB

  • Our first product Electric Pedicab, is a zero pollution electric vehicle catering the needs of end mile connectivity.

  • Use of these vehicles in fleets not only promotes usage of public transport but will help reduce air pollution and will accelerate the transition towards clean mobility.
